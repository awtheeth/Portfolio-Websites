Positive Impact Of Technology

Almost everyone is using technology for their work as well as for research. With the help of technology, each person can access information anytime and anywhere. A various field like education, internet, industries, and hospital uses technology for better output in their respective field. In education, teachers can use technology like the animated diagram and give the example of that certain course content to the student which makes student easy to learn rather than writing theoretically. Even students have a lot of sources to understand their assignment. Everything that the person wants to learn is possible with the help of technology. It also assists the student in decision-making with good presentation skills using PowerPoint. Research is regarded as an integral part of education where students can research from articles, books documents, and other reading materials. Therefore, with the help of the internet and storing facilities of technology, it makes it easy to research systematic way.

We can feel climate change and it is in an increasing phase. Different researchers have developed technology-based weather monitoring system which has monitors temperature, air pressure, humidity, and light intensity. The tsunami that will happen near shore or rainfall occurring in any place can be estimated before it happens which can save many living being's life. Wireless Sensor Network is one technology that is embedded to identify the condition of any objects which can send the radio signal of suitable amplitude and message to know the situation of the objects. It identifies the situation of traffic jam in each road and by analyzing the road condition, people can take a different route where traffic jam is less.

Every task is performed by technological equipment which has caused the loss of people's employment.
